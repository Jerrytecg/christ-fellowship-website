🌟 Vision Statement

To raise a generation of kingdom-minded believers who walk in intimacy with the Holy Spirit, manifest the power of God, and transform lives through the Word of God.

🌟 Mission Statement

Equipping believers to know God intimately, grow in spiritual maturity, and impact their spheres of influence with kingdom principles.

  • ✅ Healing and deliverance
  • ✅ Leadership and purpose discovery
  • ✅ Community and kingdom impact
